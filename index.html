Just who IS that girl?

Well, it depends who's asking. I mean, don't we all have multiple identities? I'm a curious creative who loves animals and is seeking balance in life. You know - balancing being active with the need to make a living, balancing being the parent I thought I would be with the **me** I need to be. Appreciating that with age comes wisdom, seniority, and confidence - and then embracing it. Letting go of the fear of who I'm *supposed* to be.

Non-athlete who pretends to hate running, loves to dance, takes advantage of shredding it as many powder days as possible, and keeps trying to figure out surfing.

Lifelong learner. Wannabe tech geek. Foodie. Dog mom. Human mom. Part introvert. Not Anti-vax, just Pro-information. Question asker. Semi-oblivious to pop culture.
