Who Said It?

That's right! The character who said that was:

Not quite. That line was said by:

This assignment began as a class demo where we worked on a random quote generator about cats. 🐱 For homework I discovered the Friends quote API and integrated it with my website using CSS and HTML. I also added special classes to get the "Friends" word to sort of look like the actual logo, while still maintaining the theme of my site. To extend the application, I added a section to challenge my visitors to guess who said the quote. If they click the correct character, they are rewarded with a positive statement. If they click the wrong name, they are gently informed of the correct character.

Aesthetic improvements are still needed on this page. This includes spacing, centering, fixed heights, and responsive to screen size.
